About Us

Bob's Diner brings a slice of American diner culture to Manchester, blended with the charm of British hospitality. Enjoy our unique atmosphere!

The Founder - Bob Thompson

Born and raised in Tower Hamlets, London, Bob Thompson grew up surrounded by a vibrant mix of cultures and cuisines. Inspired by the bustling food markets and his own family’s cooking, Bob dreamed of creating a place that combined the warm, inclusive vibe of his London neighborhood with the classic American diner experience.

In 2015, this dream became a reality when Bob opened the doors to Bob’s Diner in Walkden. Drawing from his diverse background, Bob has crafted a menu that offers something for everyone, ensuring that each dish carries a hint of his journey from Tower Hamlets to Walkden.

Our Menu

Check out our delicious, creatively crafted dishes below!

Manchester Maple Burger: A juicy beef patty topped with crispy bacon, cheddar cheese, and a sweet maple syrup glaze, served on a fresh brioche bun.

Walkden Waffle Wonder: Fluffy waffles topped with fresh berries, whipped cream, and a drizzle of honey.

High Street Hot Wings: Spicy buffalo wings served with celery sticks and a cooling blue cheese dip.

Salford Fish 'n' Chips: Beer-battered fish served with thick-cut chips and homemade tartar sauce.

Old Trafford Tofu Tacos: Marinated tofu with cilantro lime slaw, wrapped in soft corn tortillas, perfect for our vegetarian friends.

Chorlton Chocolate Cheesecake: Decadent chocolate cheesecake topped with a rich chocolate ganache.
